about me

Hi there! My name is Colin, I'm 23 years old, and I just finished my undergrad degree at Oxford with a bachelor's in Economics and Politics this past June. In between high school and graduating college, I worked for a year as a campaign field organizer, and also spent another year as a clerk at a small derivatives trading firm on the floor of the New York Stock Exchange.

Last summer I discovered Objective-C for iOS and OS X development on a whim, and inexplicably fell in love with it and—more explicably—programming in general.

For the past nine weeks, I've been a student at App Academy in New York, where I've built some sweet stuff with Ruby, Rails, Rspec, JavaScript, jQuery, Backbone, and Node. I'm most proficient in Ruby, but I'll always have a soft spot for my quirky, slowly-dying first language, Objective-C.
